## Loops, ranges and codes
#### Loops are very important in programming languages.
**for() loop** <br>
````
fruits = ["Apple", "Peach", "Pear"]
fruits.append("banana")
for fruit in fruits:
    print(fruit)
    print(fruit + " pie")
````
